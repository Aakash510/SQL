



## Minor Project 2:



**This project involves -**
  Analyse the market trends of the Used Car Market from multiple geographies


**Aim:** 
  Create a short research report on the Used Car Market.

**Task:**
  Understand the structure of the table and identify relevant columns for analysis.\
  Query the table to retrieve information about its structure.\

  Calculate average price by car make and model?\
  Count the number of cars based on year of manufacture?\
  Calculate Maximum Distance Travelled by Car Make and Model?


**Actions:**
